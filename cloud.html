The Cloud

Keeping us connected during the pandemic:

Allowed transition of work from in person to remote during the pandemic. Allowed children to be schooled and educated at the same time. Remote work has become part of the new normal and has become a much more common and prominent requriement for jobs seekers.

Kept families and friends together when they couldnt see each other

Cloud Computing

None of above would be possible wihout the evolution of cloud computing

Cloud computing allows work to be stored and accessed remotely, away from the office. Also allows people to meet virtually, bringing the office to your home

Meet up with your friends without leaving the house and store photos, videos and messages without needing them physcially written to your hardware

Allows recorded lessons and lectures to be accessed on demand by students without an excessive requirement of storage space
